# AI Search Beauty Discovery — Target Scorecard

Date: 2026-04-03
Primary wedge: Seoul / Gangnam laser hair removal clinics

## Goal
Prioritize prospects who are most likely to:
1. feel the pain
2. be easy to onboard
3. show measurable outcomes quickly
4. pay for pilot work

## Scoring dimensions
Each target gets 0–3 per dimension.

### 1. Comparison-intent fit
- 3: treatment has strong comparison behavior (price, area, branch, sessions)
- 2: some comparison behavior
- 1: low comparison
- 0: mostly branding / loyalty-driven

### 2. Source readiness
- 3: clear official pricing page or treatment page exists
- 2: info exists across site + socials
- 1: mostly social-only
- 0: no usable public sources

### 3. AI/search sensitivity
- 3: likely searched in AI / English / tourist / high-research audience
- 2: moderate search behavior
- 1: low search reliance
- 0: mostly offline referrals

### 4. Ease of extraction
- 3: clear branch, treatment, price, CTA already public
- 2: minor cleanup needed
- 1: lots of ambiguity
- 0: too messy for fast pilot

### 5. Commercial likelihood
- 3: premium clinic / active marketing / multiple branches / visible digital presence
- 2: medium digital maturity
- 1: weak digital maturity
- 0: poor fit for paid pilot

### 6. Measurability
- 3: official booking/contact URL can be tracked easily
- 2: at least one measurable CTA exists
- 1: limited tracking path
- 0: no clear CTA

## Total score bands
- 15–18: top-priority pilot target
- 11–14: good backup target
- 7–10: maybe later
- 0–6: avoid for first wave

## Best first-wave traits
Look for clinics that have:
- public English or bilingual pricing pages
- Seoul / Gangnam location pages
- laser hair removal listed explicitly
- branch-level URLs
- booking/contact links
- heavy consumer comparison behavior

## Avoid in first wave
- no public pricing or no useful official details
- highly regulated claims-heavy pages with vague facts only
- providers with no clear booking/contact path
- businesses that would require complex legal or medical content interpretation upfront

## Suggested first-wave prospect categories
1. Gangnam clinics with public laser pricing
2. bilingual clinics targeting foreign patients
3. clinics with multiple branches and branch-specific pages
4. clinics already running active event / package pages

## Data to collect for each prospect
- brand / clinic name
- branch
- city/district
- homepage URL
- pricing URL
- booking URL
- Instagram URL
- languages available
- target treatment(s)
- score by dimension
- notes / observed pain clues
