### Strategic Verdict

**For a small online butcher shop:**
1. **한우 premium**: Use as brand anchor, gift sets, holiday peaks. Higher absolute margins on 갈비/안심/목심. Requires "한우" certification and clear grade labeling.
2. **호주산/미국산 imported**: Best for volume bulgogi/bulgogi-set products. Lower buy-in cost, more consistent supply, good margins (~35–45%). Suitable for 불고기용, 스테이크, 부채살.
3. **국내산 육우**: Price fighter tier. 1,680원/100g앞다리 is the cheapest option online. Good for acquiring price-sensitive customers, cross-sell to higher SKUs.

Meatlovernews confirms: large conglomerates (Dong-won, Daesang, Hyundai) are entering online meat with **vertical platform / niche targeting** strategies. Small shops must own a specific identity (origin story, local farm, specific grade specialty) rather than compete on pure price.

## [LIMITATION]

1. **Factory gate prices (KMTA) are single-point readings** — they vary by ~10–20% by company and fluctuate weekly. No time-series was available.
2. **Butcher buy price markup (15%)** is an assumption based on industry structure, not a directly measured figure. The actual spread varies by volume, contract, and supplier relationship.
3. **Online retail prices** were collected from 4 sources (Coupang, Emart, Lotte Zetta, search metadata) on a single date. Dynamic pricing means these can shift 10–15% within weeks.
4. **Demand scores** in the strategic matrix (Fig 3) are qualitative, derived from search result ranking and editorial judgment — not quantitative sales data (actual SmartStore sales volumes were not accessible).
5. **육우 and imported wholesale prices** were not directly confirmed — only retail prices observed. Margin estimates for these categories are therefore wider-range approximations.
6. **Online market share (9.2%)** is sourced from a 2024 meatlovernews article citing Korean Rural Economic Institute; the 2026 figure may be higher given "home-grill" trend acceleration.
7. **Regional variation**: KAMIS shows up to 36% price spread for the same cut across Korean regions (e.g., 안심 13,054–17,779원/100g). Online prices represent a national average.
