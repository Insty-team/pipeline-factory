## 4. 성공 사례 (Success Stories)

### Case 1: 정육각 (JeongYookGak) — Online-first butcher
- **Model:** "Slaughtered within 4 days" freshness guarantee, farm-to-door
- **Revenue:** KRW 40 billion/year (2021-2022 peak)
- **Launch:** KRW 5,000,000 initial capital → KRW 20M/month revenue within 3 months
- **Key differentiator:** Simplified supply chain (farm > slaughterhouse > JeongYookGak)
- **Warning:** Raised KRW 120 billion in VC funding, expanded aggressively (acquired Chorokmaul), entered bankruptcy proceedings 2025 due to debt burden — not an operational failure
- [STAT:n] Public financial records, ZDNet Korea reporting

### Case 2: Premium Gangnam Butcher (Anonymous, i-boss.co.kr)
- **Revenue:** KRW 140M/month achieved within first month of online expansion
- **Hero product:** 꽃등심 at KRW 16,900/100g (premium positioning, NOT competing on price)
- **Strategy:** Renamed cuts by customer desire (e.g., "high-protein ribeye for your child")
- **Key insight:** 70% of purchase decision is emotional/presentation; taste is only 30%
- [STAT:n] Single operator case study

### Case 3: Goggi Now (고기나우) — Neighborhood butcher delivery platform
- **Model:** 1-hour delivery from local butchers within 3km radius
- **Growth:** +194% MoM transaction growth; +163% MoM member growth (early stage)
- **Consumer value:** Custom thickness/weight/marbling specifications
- [STAT:n] Sedaily.com platform-level report

### Case 4: Standard Offline Butcher Shop Benchmarks
- **Average monthly revenue:** KRW 30-40M (national average)
- **Net profit (typical):** KRW 4-5M/month on KRW 30M revenue (16% net)
- **Gross margin on meat:** 30-50% (average ~40%)
- **With online channel:** Top performers reach KRW 60M+ net/month (combined shop + restaurant)
- [STAT:n] National tax statistics, DH Money blog analysis

## 6. 포장 및 배송 요건 (Packaging & Delivery Requirements)

### Legal Temperature Requirements (식품위생법 기준)
- **냉장 생육:** -2°C ~ +10°C during storage and transport (가금류: -2 ~ +5°C)
- **냉동육:** -18°C 이하
- **법적 근거:** 식품의약품안전처 고시, 식품의 기준 및 규격

### Recommended Packaging Protocol

| Component | Specification | Unit Cost |
|-----------|---------------|-----------|
| 스티로폼 박스 (2cm+ thickness) | Min. 2cm wall thickness | KRW 1,280-2,600/unit |
| 아이스팩 (gel packs) | 2 units minimum | KRW 330/unit (bulk) = KRW 660 |
| 진공 포장 (vacuum seal) | Per meat portion | ~KRW 200-300/unit |
| 골판지 외부 박스 | Over-wrap for shipping | ~KRW 400-600/unit |
| **Total packaging cost** | Per shipment | **KRW 2,000-2,500** |

### Effective Cooling Duration
- Gel ice packs only (x2): Up to 48 hours at ≤10°C (tested, adequate for next-day delivery)
- Dry ice + gel packs: 24 hours with superior temperature maintenance (recommended for summer)
- Dry ice only: 24 hours, best for frozen product

### Delivery Constraints
- 일반 택배 (standard courier): Next-day delivery possible with proper packaging
- 새벽 배송 (dawn delivery): Requires platform partnership (Kurly, Coupang Rocket Fresh)
- 당일 배송 (same-day): Requires own driver or hyperlocal platform (Goggi Now, MeatQ)

[FINDING] Standard Korean courier (택배) with gel ice packs is legally sufficient and cost-effective for next-day delivery of refrigerated raw beef. Dry ice is mandatory only for frozen products.
[STAT:n] Based on MFDS guidelines and cold chain experiment data (물류신문)

## 7. 핵심 전략 권고 (Strategic Recommendations)

### For a Rural Butcher Shop Entering Online

1. **Launch channel: Naver SmartStore first** — lowest fee (5%), largest reach, food-friendly
   - Alternative: self-hosted site for zero commission but requires marketing spend

2. **Anchor SKU: 앞다리 불고기용 500g / 1kg bundles**
   - Highest volume, familiar to consumers, accessible price point (KRW 18,000-21,000/500g)

3. **Revenue SKU: 갈비 (short ribs) set**
   - 41% gross margin, strong holiday/weekend demand

4. **Differentiation: "산지 직송" (direct from farm) narrative**
   - Rural origin is an ASSET, not a liability — consumers pay premium for traceability

5. **Realistic volume targets:**
   - Month 1-3: 50-100 orders/month (KRW 2-4M revenue)
   - Month 6-12: 200-400 orders/month (KRW 8-16M revenue) if marketing is consistent
   - Top operators: KRW 30-50M/month online (requires 2+ years, strong reviews)

6. **Critical unit economics threshold:**
   - Orders below KRW 30,000 are margin-negative after logistics on SmartStore
   - Minimum viable order: 1kg beef (KRW 35,000-42,000+) to achieve positive net margin

[LIMITATION]
1. Wholesale prices from KMTA are Grade 1 averages; rural butchers may source at 5-15% premium over these prices depending on location and supplier relationships.
2. Online retail prices are midpoint estimates from public listings; actual realized prices vary significantly by platform, season, and promotion.
3. The 500g margin model assumes 50% raw material cost, which is conservative for higher grades (1+, 1++) where raw cost may reach 55-60% of retail.
4. Volume projections (Month 1-12) are extrapolated from qualitative case studies, not longitudinal data — high variance expected.
5. The online meat market has seen significant platform competition from large players (Coupang, SSG, Kurly) which creates pricing pressure; small sellers must differentiate on quality/story, not price.
6. 정육각's bankruptcy (2025) illustrates that even well-funded online meat startups can fail — success requires operational discipline, not just market entry.
7. This research relied on publicly available Korean web sources as of April 2026; actual regulatory requirements should be verified with the local 식품의약품안전처 office.
