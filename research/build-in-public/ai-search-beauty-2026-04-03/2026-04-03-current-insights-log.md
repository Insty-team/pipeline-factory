Purpose: preserve the important conclusions from this conversation so we can resume without re-litigating them.

## 1. What we were testing
Primary short-term idea:
- Help beauty / medical-aesthetics providers get their official information represented more accurately in AI/search flows.

Initial framing that was too weak:
- “AI-friendly page 제작”

Refined framing that is stronger:
- “정확한 구조화 + 검수 + 공개 배포 + 추적”

## 2. Critical insight
A simple “AI-friendly page” service is not differentiated enough.
Reason:
- ChatGPT or a coding agent can already read a URL and summarize it.
- If our offer is just “we make a nicer page,” customers may not pay.

The stronger differentiated unit is:
1. source-grounded fact extraction
2. canonicalization across multiple official URLs
3. provider approval / review
4. public deployment as a crawlable surface
5. measurement / tracking over time

## 3. Business model insight
The beauty-clinic structured-discovery idea may be:
- realistic
- monetizable
- but probably not a fast, explosive, viral business

Likely characteristics:
- B2B
- sales-heavy
- trust-heavy
- slower proof cycle
- more service-like in the beginning

Therefore it may be a:
- good “slow realistic” path
- but not necessarily a “1–7 day breakout” path

## 4. Decision for now
We should not mix many hypotheses at once.
For the next month, if we keep pursuing the clinic angle, it should be tested narrowly.

Narrow hypothesis chosen:
> 병원은 “AI-friendly page 제작”보다 “정확한 구조화 + 검수 + 공개 배포 + 추적” 묶음을 더 가치 있는 제안으로 인식하고, 파일럿 논의로 전환할 가능성이 높다.
